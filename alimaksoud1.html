Our mission is to use coding and digital skills to enable inclusion and employment, and as a catalyst for social innovation and diversity. We are a combination between a coding bootcamp and a leadership course, based in Beirut.
Our aim is to get marginalised youth to participate in the growing needs of the local ICT and software sector.

Codi is a free school of programming for people who have a passion for digital and want to build a career in technology but cannot pursue a traditional university education.

Core Web Development 6-month Course

Our Core Web Development course offers a free, intensive 6-month programme to become full-stack web developers (front and back end), digital project managers & many other digital professions which are in high demand today.

Computer Literacy 2-month Course

Our latest course (launching April 2020) offers a free, 2-month programme aimed at marginalised youth who want to learn basic computer skills for employment and business purposes
